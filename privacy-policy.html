Privacy Policy for GCP Project Colorizer

Last updated: November 20, 2025

Data Collection

GCP Project Colorizer does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. We do not track your browsing activity, collect personal information, or share any data with third parties.

Local Storage

This extension stores user-configured project-to-color mappings locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data includes only the project IDs and color values that you explicitly configure. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: Used to store your project-to-color mappings locally on your device.
  • Host permission (console.cloud.google.com): Required to read the active project ID from the GCP Console page and apply the configured banner color. No data is collected from these pages.

No External Communication

This extension does not communicate with any external servers. All functionality operates entirely within your browser and on the GCP Console pages you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or the extension's GitHub repository.
